HDPoints

HDPoints:


For every 10 minutes you spend watching the stream, you receive 5 HDPoints.


HDPoints can be used for entering giveaways, gambling, joining whitelisted servers, and (coming soon) rankup system.


HD_Bot Commands:


"!hdpoints" or "!currency" (Displays how many points you currently have)


"!hdpoints top5" or "!currency top5" (Displays the top 5 users with the most points)


"!time" (Displays how much time you have spent in the stream)


"!uptime" (Displays how long I have been live for that stream)
